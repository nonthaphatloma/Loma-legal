Privacy Policy for Loma

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Loma ("we," "our," or "us") provides a service that schedules and posts short-form videos to TikTok on behalf of its users. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information. When you connect your TikTok account to Loma via TikTok's OAuth login, we receive basic profile information such as your TikTok username, display name, and profile photo.

Authorization Tokens. We store the access and refresh tokens issued by TikTok so that Loma can post content to your account on your behalf. These tokens are encrypted at rest.

Content You Upload. We collect and temporarily store the video files, captions, thumbnails, and scheduling information you provide in order to publish them to your TikTok account at the time you specify.

Usage Data. We may collect basic technical information (such as device type, log data, and app interaction events) to maintain and improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account and maintain your session
  • Schedule and publish content to TikTok on your behalf, per your instructions
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Monitor, maintain, and improve the reliability and security of the service
  • Comply with legal obligations and TikTok's Developer Terms of Service

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your TikTok content or account data for advertising purposes.

3. How We Share Your Information

We share information only as follows:

  • With TikTok, as necessary to authenticate your account and publish content you have scheduled, via TikTok's official APIs.
  • With service providers who help us operate Loma (e.g., cloud hosting, storage), under confidentiality obligations, and only to the extent necessary to provide the service.
  • As required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Loma, our users, or others.

We do not share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account connection and content only for as long as necessary to provide the service. Uploaded video files are deleted from our systems after successful publication to TikTok, or after a limited retention period if publication fails. You may disconnect your TikTok account and request deletion of your data at any time.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Disconnect your TikTok account from Loma at any time, which revokes our access to post on your behalf
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard technical and organizational measures, including encryption of stored authorization tokens, to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

7. Children's Privacy

Loma is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of Loma after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
