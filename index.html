Loma is a tool that schedules and posts short-form videos to TikTok on behalf of the connected user. Creators link their TikTok account, upload the videos they want to publish, and choose when each one should go live — Loma handles the posting automatically at the scheduled time.

Loma only publishes content to the TikTok account that a user has explicitly connected and authorized. No content is ever posted without the account owner's direct authorization via TikTok's official login flow.
